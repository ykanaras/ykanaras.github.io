About Us

Bear, Delaware • Since 2002

Welcome to Trattoria Di Napoli, where authentic Italian cooking meets the heart of Bear, Delaware. Our owner, born and raised near Naples—the birthplace of pizza—brings his passion for traditional Italian cuisine to every dish we serve.

From hand-tossed pizzas and house-made pastas to rustic entrées and homemade desserts, our menu reflects the flavors of true Italian cooking.

At Trattoria Di Napoli, you’re not just enjoying a meal—you’re part of our famiglia. Buon appetito!
