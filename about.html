Welcome to Trattoria Di Napoli Restaurant - the right place for delicious pizza and food in Bear, DE!

Established in 2002, we're an independent restaurant offering outstanding dishes and food to all customers in the community. Our owners are from a small town near Naples, the birthplace of pizza. Hence, we bring that extra touch of authenticity to everything we do. Stop by for lunch or dinner or opt for pickup or delivery.

That said, let's take a closer look at our menu, starting with the pizza selection. Enjoy our classic or Sicilian Cheese pie, or even create your own with the toppings you prefer.

Next, we serve a number of specialty combinations. Order our traditional Margherita with fresh mozzarella, basil, sauce, and parmesan cheese. Likewise, we suggest our popular Combo pizza. It comes with pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, green peppers, onions, and extra cheese.

For vegetarians, our Veggie pizza is the perfect choice. We make it with broccoli, mushrooms, green peppers, onions, and tomatoes. Plus, you can pick between red or white sauce.

Take your taste buds on an adventure with our fabulous Hawaiian pie. It's topped with pineapple and ham, also with a choice of red or white sauce.

In addition, we recommend our famous Meat Lovers pizza with sausage, pepperoni, ham, and meatballs.

Start your meal with one of our appetizers or bruschetta options. Indulge in our fried calamari, prosciutto & mozzarella Caprese, or the awesome bruschetta sampler, to name a few.

Moreover, we have various side dishes such as French fries, meatballs, roasted peppers, vegetable medley, and others.

For something fresh, try any of our delicious salads. Whether it's our Caesar, Greek, or Chef salad, you cannot go wrong with any.

As a great substitute for pizza, grab our classic calzone with ricotta and mozzarella. We also have several stromboli options including our American stromboli with steak, pepperoni, and mozzarella.

Furthermore, our menu features different cold subs, burgers, paninis, and wraps. These include our Italian sub, cheeseburger, grilled chicken Caesar wrap, and more.

In addition, we serve some great-tasting steak and rustic dishes such as our Philly cheesesteak, pizza steak, chicken rustic, and porketta rustic, among others.

On top of that, we offer a variety of pasta, baked, chicken, veal, and seafood dishes. Enjoy our spaghetti, meat lasagna, chicken parmigiana, our famous seafood pescatore, and more.

For desserts, grab a classic New York cheesecake or indulge in some incredible tiramisu or cannoli.

We look forward to having you as a guest at our awesome restaurant. You can also order online for pickup or delivery, and we even have great catering options for any special event. See you soon!
